The Mastercad Backend API gives you programmatic access to a curated database of fintech companies, AI-generated market research reports, and one-click presentation exports. Authenticate once with a Supabase JWT token, then query companies, generate reports, and export to PPTX or Gamma — all through clean REST endpoints.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Obtain a JWT token">
    Call `POST /v1/auth/login` with your email and password to receive a Supabase access token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the Authorization header">
    Include `Authorization: Bearer <your_token>` on every subsequent request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Query companies">
    Call `POST /v1/companies/filter` with optional filters to retrieve a paginated list of fintech companies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a market research report">
    Call `POST /v1/market-research` with a topic and up to five company IDs to produce an AI-written report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The API base URL is `http://localhost:8000` for local development. All endpoints are prefixed with `/v1`.
</Note>
